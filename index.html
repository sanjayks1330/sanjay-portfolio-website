About Me

My journey into the world of data began with a powerful conviction: the belief that insights derived from data can fundamentally transform understanding and drive impactful solutions. As I pursued my MSc in Data Science at Ulster University, I became fascinated by how machine learning, statistical modeling, and robust data visualization could reveal patterns and unlock new possibilities.

During my academic endeavors, I led a significant project, "An Influential Stock Market Paradigm based on LSTM Modeling and Sequencing." Here, I applied LSTM models to analyze stock market time-series data, predicting new attributes and rigorously evaluating results using Google Colab and Python. This experience honed my coding skills and solidified my practical understanding of data analysis, machine learning libraries, databases, and SQL, complemented by front-end knowledge in JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3.

Currently, as an IT Support Engineer, I leverage my analytical mindset daily to solve real-world problems. This role provides invaluable hands-on experience in understanding complex systems and troubleshooting technical challenges, reinforcing the importance of data-driven problem-solving. My background in data science enables me to approach IT issues with a systematic perspective, always seeking not just to fix, but to understand underlying patterns and improve processes.

Outside of Professional Pursuits

Football has been a significant part of my life, having played at both state and district levels in India for over a decade and serving as captain of my University College team, which taught me invaluable lessons about leadership and resilience. Beyond the pitch, I find joy in traveling, socializing, and embracing new adventures. I am eager to combine my strong foundation in data science with practical IT expertise to drive innovation and create real impact.
